Click any tract or reservation for population counts. The control panel (upper left) toggles tract coloring (urban–rural type, share of national AIAN population, or local AIAN concentration), state lines, and tribal lands. The buffer slider expands each reservation by up to 20 miles and recomputes population counts to include people living within the buffered area. Note: a person living near multiple reservations is counted toward each of them, so buffered population totals do not sum cleanly across reservations — they are intended to answer "how many people live within X miles of this reservation." Source: 2020 Decennial Census (NHGIS), USDA ERS RUCA codes (2020), and TIGER/Line. Excludes Puerto Rico, other US territories, and Hawaiian Home Lands.
